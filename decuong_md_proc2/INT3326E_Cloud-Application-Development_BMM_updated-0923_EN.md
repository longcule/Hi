<strong>Summary of</strong> <strong>course content </strong></p><p>The course introduces students to the basic principles of e-commerce application development. Students will be instructed to sign up for a user-specific account for free application development on several popular e-commerce platforms such as Amazon AWS, Microsoft Azure, SalesForce, and Office 365. The course will introduce each of these platforms and teach students how to deploy software on them. At the end of the course, students will be able to develop and deploy software on nowadays market-leading e-commerce-based systems.</p><p><strong>6.
